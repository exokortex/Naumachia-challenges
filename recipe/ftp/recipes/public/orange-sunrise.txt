      Title: Orange Sunrise
 Categories: Beverages, Alcohol-no
      Yield: 3 servings

      2    Eggs
    1/4 c  Frozen Orange Juice
           -Concentrate
      2 T  Honey
      2 c  Cold Milk
      8 oz Orange flavoured yogurt

Combine the eggs, orange juice and honey in the blender.
Blend until smooth (about 1 minute).

Add the milk and yougurt.

Blend until smooth and frothy.

Serve at once in tall, chilled glass.
